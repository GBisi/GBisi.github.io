Jacopo Soldani received the PhD degree in Computer Science in 2017 from the University of Pisa (Italy) with a thesis on "Modelling, analysing and reusing composite cloud applications". He is now an assistant professor (RTDa) at the Department of Computer Science of the University of Pisa. His current research interests include services, cloud, and fog computing, with a particular focus on microservices, cloud-native applications, and faults and fault-resilience. He has been involved in multiple research projects on the orchestration of service-based applications on cloud and fog platforms. He is also member of the editorial board of internationally renowned journals (including ACM SIGSOFT SEN and PLOS ONE) and he has also participated to the organisation of various editions of international conferences and workshops (e.g., CLOSER, CoopIS, ESOCC, and ESEC/FSE).
