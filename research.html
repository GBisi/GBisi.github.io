Research Interests

I am part of the Service-Oriented, Cloud and Fog Computing (SOCC) research group. My research interests include, but are not limited to, what follows.
  • Software Engineering for Quantum Computing
  • Quantum Computing, Software Engineering, Hybrid Classical-Quantum Cloud & Services, Hybrid Classical-Quantum Ecosystems, Service Engineering, Quantum Languages & Abstractions.
  • Orchestration of Multi-Service Applications over the Cloud-Edge Continuum
  • Multi-Service Application & Microservices, Cloud-Edge Continuum, IoT Devices, Continuous Management & Continuous Reasoning, Declarative Methodologies, QoS-aware Orchestration, Monitoring of Geographically Distributed Systems.
  • Management of Smart Environments
  • Declarative Frameworks for Smart Environments, Customisable Policies, Goal-oriented methodologies, Management of Contrasting Goals and Actuators Settings, Sustainable Objectives.
